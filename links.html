The Artist Formally Known As...


Devoted Brother, Son, Niece, Nephew, Granddaughter, Sister, Aunt, Uncle, Boyfriend, Lover, Friend, Partner
Arist and performer Eros C Backus, pictured in
collaborator Mads Reardons' studio (Mads Reardon)

        The artist formally known as Eros C(arl) Backus is a craftsman of many trades- and hats for that matter.
Forever interested in the hierarchies of materials and their processes, he recreates and investigates mundane, everyday objects
to better understand and subsequently deconstruct the values they hold in society.
  Never truly focused on exact mimicry, Eros continually plays with form and (non)function intertwined with traditional histories of mediums.
Much of his work centers around activation and performance that displays tangible humor and a proclivity for showmanship,
as he is seemingly unable to leave an object by itself. In his current series of works that he lovingly describes as 'prop work',
he demonstrates a great knowledge of timing and intention with the eye of a director.
  While there is a certain level of play in Eros' works there is a deeper remembering of nostalgia and memory at hand as well;
a calculated catharsis that he, as a performer and artist goes through, as does the viewer of his work. Tactility is extremely prevelant,
physically and emotionally, whether that is in weight, material, or memory.


                                            Eros especially will be remembered for his tendency towards the dramatic.
