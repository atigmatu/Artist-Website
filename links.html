The Artist Formally Known As...


Devoted Brother, Son, Niece, Nephew, Granddaughter, Sister, Aunt, Uncle, Boyfriend, Lover, Friend, Partner

        The artist formally known as Eros Carl Backus, was born on February 9th, 2001, in Lansing, Michigan as the town his family
lived in at the time did not have a hospital (and still doesn’t).
        The youngest of six, they grew up as a pastor's kid in rural Illinois spending most of their summers in northern Minnesota
where his Nana taught them various fiber crafts and gardening skills.
        Primarily a ceramic artist (or at least in his heart of hearts) Eros has worked in slip casted porcelain with what he has called
“prop work”-molding mass produced objects to be broken as an act of catharsis or calculated failure. These performances lean into
considering the complexity of queerness through domestic and industrialized materials/processes and antiquated hierarchical values.
Their work draws from personal experiences and seemingly unable to leave an object by itself, he focuses on playing with materiality
of items with humor that shows a proclivity for showmanship.


                                            Eros especially will be remembered for his tendency towards the dramatic.
